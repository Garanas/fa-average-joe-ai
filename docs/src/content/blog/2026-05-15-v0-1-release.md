# v0.1 — first playable release

The first tagged release of the Average Joe AI is out. The AI now boots cleanly on any 10km map, picks a sensible starting build order, and stops trying to put a mass extractor on top of its own factory.

## Highlights

- **Base chunks** replace the hand-coded build orders that v0 had stitched together over six months. One template, four factions, no copy-paste drift.
- **Engineers actually reclaim wrecks** now. Previous behaviour: walked past them on the way to a job. Current behaviour: stops, reclaims, then resumes the job.
- **Retreat phase** no longer leaves orphaned platoons standing around. The base coordinator releases them back to the brain's roaming pool, which re-tasks them within a tick.

## Known issues

A few rough edges that did not make the cut:

1. The AI occasionally builds two adjacent T1 land factories instead of one T1 + one T2. Cosmetic, not a blocker.
2. On maps with very narrow chokepoints, the base chunk placement can fail to find a valid tile and silently skip the chunk. We log the skip; the AI keeps playing.
3. Cybran stealth is not yet exploited in the build order — coming in v0.2.

## Try it

Drop the mod into your FAF custom mods folder, restart the client, and tick *Average Joe AI* in the lobby. Pick any AI slot.
