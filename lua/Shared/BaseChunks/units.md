## How to interpret the columns

- `SkirtSizeX` / `SkirtSizeZ` — the **footprint that matters for chunk planning**. This is the rectangle the engine reserves around the structure on the build grid; no other structure may overlap it. **Use these for placing buildings inside a chunk.**
- `SkirtOffsetX` / `SkirtOffsetZ` — offset of the skirt rectangle from the unit's center. Combined with the skirt size this gives the actual occupied cells.
- `FootprintX` / `FootprintZ` — the smaller rectangle inside the skirt that the structure physically blocks. The ring between the footprint and the skirt is walkable terrain that other units can pass through but where no structure can be built. Mostly informational for our purposes.

In short: the _skirt_ is the keep-out box used for placement; the _footprint_ is the solid mesh inside it. For authoring base chunks we care about the skirt fields.
